Breaking Barriers: Strategies for Handling Societal Pressure in Engineering

SPEAKER

Engr. Jenina Camille Abag

Insights...

As an engineering student, "Breaking Barriers: Strategies for Handling Societal Pressure in Engineering" is a seminar that delves into the unique challenges and societal expectations faced by individuals in the engineering field. This insightful session explores effective strategies to navigate and manage external pressures, providing practical tools for overcoming obstacles that may arise throughout one's engineering journey. The seminar aims to equip participants with valuable insights on maintaining mental well-being and achieving personal and professional success amid societal expectations.

For me, a Mechatronics Engineering student, this topic holds particular relevance. The advantage lies in gaining a nuanced understanding of the external pressures that may impact not only my academic and professional pursuits but also my overall well-being. By learning effective strategies to handle societal expectations, I can develop a resilient mindset, enabling me to focus on my studies, navigate career choices, and contribute meaningfully to the field of Mechatronics Engineering. Ultimately, this seminar empowers me to thrive in a demanding environment, fostering a balanced and successful engineering journey.
